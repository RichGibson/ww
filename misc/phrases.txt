The Piston Bot provides the opportunity to play with pneumatic control to generate a 
cocktail accompanied with a musical interlude.  Using the latest developments in 
electro-mechanical-pneumatic & tactile musical controls allows for an 
exploration of the incremental value added to the cocktail production process by the 
addition of musical accompanient.
